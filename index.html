Privacy Policy for Frequency Generator

Last Updated: December 10, 2024

Quick Summary: We only store your custom sound configurations (frequencies, waveforms, volumes). We do NOT collect any personal information, health data, location data, or usage analytics.

1. Information We Collect

When you choose to save custom sounds in the Frequency Generator app, we store:

  • Sound Configurations: The frequencies, waveforms, volumes, and layer settings you create
  • Sound Names: The names you give to your saved sounds
  • Timestamps: When you created each saved sound

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect, store, or process:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Health or medical data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Biometric data
  • Any information that could identify you personally

3. How We Use Your Data

The sound configurations you save are used exclusively to:

  • Display your saved sounds in the Library section of the app
  • Allow you to replay your custom sound configurations
  • Enable you to edit or delete your saved sounds at any time

4. Data Storage and Security

Your saved sound configurations are stored on secure servers. These configurations are:

  • Stored only when you explicitly choose to save a sound
  • Not linked to any personal identifying information
  • Accessible only through the app on your device
  • Protected using industry-standard security measures
Your saved sound configurations are stored securely on our backend servers. We maintain the same privacy protections for this data as outlined throughout this policy. Our backend infrastructure: - Uses industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage - Does not share your data with any third-party services - Stores only anonymous sound settings (frequencies, waveforms, volumes) - Does not link saved sounds to any personal identifiers No third parties have access to your stored sound configurations.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Saved sounds remain stored until you choose to delete them
  • You can delete individual saved sounds at any time through the Library section
  • Deletion is immediate and permanent
  • We do not retain copies of deleted sound configurations

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade your sound configurations or any other data with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for general entertainment and audio exploration purposes.

8. App Purpose and Disclaimer

Important: Frequency Generator is designed for entertainment and audio exploration purposes only. It is NOT intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. It is NOT a medical device or health app. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for any medical concerns.

9. User Consent

When you save a sound for the first time, the app requests your consent to store sound configurations. You can choose to:

  • Accept: Allow the app to save your custom sound configurations
  • Decline: Use the app without saving sounds (you can still create and play sounds in real-time)

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

You have the right to:

  • Access your saved sound configurations (viewable in the Library section)
  • Delete your saved sounds at any time
  • Choose not to save any sounds and use the app in real-time only
  • Stop using the app at any time
