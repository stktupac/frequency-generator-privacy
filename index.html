Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Frequency Generator. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect only the sound frequency configurations that you choose to save within the app. This includes:

  • Frequency values (in Hz)
  • Waveform types (sine, square, triangle, sawtooth)
  • Volume levels
  • Layer configurations (multiple sound layers)
  • Names you assign to saved sounds
Important: All saved data is stored anonymously. We do not collect or require any personal information to use this app.

Information We DO NOT Collect

We are committed to your privacy. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address, etc.)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Health or medical data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers beyond what's necessary for the app to function
  • Contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Biometric data
  • Payment information (the app is free)

How We Use Your Information

The saved sound configurations are stored on our servers solely to:

  • Allow you to access your saved sounds across app sessions
  • Enable you to retrieve your previously created sound configurations
  • Provide the core functionality of saving and loading sound presets

Data Storage and Security

Your saved sounds are stored securely on our database servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Your Rights and Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Individual Sounds: You can delete any saved sound from your library at any time
  • Delete All Data: You can permanently delete all your data using the "Delete All My Data" button in the Settings screen
  • No Account Required: Since we don't collect personal information, there's no account to manage

Data Retention

We retain your saved sound configurations until you choose to delete them. When you delete data (either individual sounds or all your data), it is permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app does not target children and does not collect personal information from any users.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your data with any third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy when changes are made.
